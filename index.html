About

Formed in 2020, Colorado Junction String Band has become a standout act in Colorado's vibrant music scene. Rooted in bluegrass, Americana, and folk, the band brings an energetic yet soulful vibe to every performance. Their sound blends traditional string instruments with tight harmonies, creating a dynamic and engaging experience for listeners of all ages.

Since their formation, they've performed at numerous venues and festivals along Colorado's Front Range, captivating audiences from Denver to Fort Collins, Boulder to Colorado Springs. Their passion for music and love for Colorado's scenic landscapes shine through in every note they play.
