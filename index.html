About

Colorado Junction is a mountain Americana band rooted in the Rocky Mountain spirit. Drawing from the towering peaks, sweeping high plains, and wild rivers of Colorado, the band crafts original music that sounds like wide open sky and deep pine forests. Rich harmonies, acoustic instruments, and storytelling songwriting make every performance feel like a campfire under the stars.

From honky-tonk to folk to bluegrass and beyond, Colorado Junction rides the rails between genres with a sound that is distinctly and proudly Colorado.
